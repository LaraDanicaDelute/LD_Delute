Hidiliyn Diaz

As a teenager Hidilyn found her passion on weightlifting, at an early age she started training with a desire of winning an olympic medal someday despite of her parent's objection on her chosen path.

Finally on 2008 Summer Olympics Diaz was chosen to to represent the Philippines in weightlifting making her the first Filipino woman to compete for the country, despite being placed second to last she was praised for her performance. In 2016 Summer Olympics,Diaz competed in the women's 53 kilogram weightlifting category and won the silver medal and she the first woman to win an Olympic medal in the country.

Despite not being able to win the gold medal on the previous Olympics she did not question herself and continue to work and train hard until, the 2020 Summer Olympics in Tokyo where she won the gold medal for weightlifting category, after many years of hardship she finally harvest the fruits of all her sacrifices.

Hidilyn Diaz received numerous awards from the government and praises from the Filipino people, Diaz has said that it is not only her work that made her win but also the efforts of her team and her faith to God. she became an inspiration to her fellow weightlifters and to young girls who aspire to be a weightlifter to continue their dreams despite of all the loses and little recognition on the sport.
