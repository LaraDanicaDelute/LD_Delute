When it all started

Juan Dela Cruz, started Pizza Area in the streets of Roxas, Davao City back in 2000, slowly he earned the recognition for his delicious, mouth watering pizza after 2 years of experimenting to ge the right combination of flavors, with his wife Marites, they added a few more item on their menu and successfully launch the second branch of Pizza Area in Bajada, Davao City.
