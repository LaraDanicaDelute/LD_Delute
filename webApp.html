Learn Davao

Learn Davao is a web application that helps spread reliable information to Davaoenos to avoid the belief of fake news about the pandemic and the vaccine. The web application aims to help prevent the rise of COVID 19 cases by dispersing information and awareness.

SOLUTIONS

Feature

Davao Learn will provide timely updates regarding the city's COVID-19 status, such as: Number of cases, High-risk areas, new implementations and, additional health tips and guide.

Reliable Source

To avoid the spread of fake news, we make sure that the data provided is from the City Government

Navigation

Davao Learn is solely for Davao City, it is designed for easy navigation such as searching specific baranggay or any information, we also aim to give users grapic presentations of data for easy understanding.

Differentiation

Why Learn Davao is different? simple, It's because our application is the first COVID-19 information source website proposed in the city as we know of. We provide reliable information and relay them in a graphical presentation which can be easily understood. Our Web application focus only in local area and, cannot be covered up by unrelated COVID-19 information.

Benefits

Who will benefit from the web application? The answer will be direct and simple, The Davao City Government, the Davaoenos and the city itself.

Government

with the existence of Learn Davao, the spread of false information is less one thing for the Government to worry about, it will aloshelp increase the awareness of the people about the current situation.

The City

The widespread use of Learn Davao will help the city in general, in a way that the wrong beliefs of the people about the vaccine and the virus may be corrected with a reliable information source.

The Davaoenos

The people of Davao don't have to rely on facebook or any social media site for COVID-19 information anymore where false information is widespread, with Learn Davao all information are guaranteed riliable.
